COMPUTER-BASED MEDICAL GUIDELINES AND PROTOCOLS

This book series was started in 1990 to promote research conducted under the auspices of the EC programmesâ€™ Advanced Informatics in Medicine (AIM) and Biomedical and Health Research (BHR) bioengineering branch. A driving aspect of international health informatics is that telecommunication technology, rehabilitative technology, intelligent home technology and many other components are moving together and form one integrated world of information and communication media. The complete series has been accepted in Medline. Volumes from 2005 onwards are available online.
